Now you can configure the **Low credits threshold**. Once your credits reach lower than this number all the _team members_ will start getting notified on email about the low credits. This email will be sent in one hour intervals whenever being consumed. Configure it from [here](https://crawlchat.app/settings#low-credits-threshold)
